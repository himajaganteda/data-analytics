## Question 1: Which Countries Underperform in Sales?

### Finding

Portugal generated the highest sales revenue, while Italy recorded the lowest sales revenue among all countries.

### Key Metrics

* Total Countries: 15
* Highest Sales Country: Portugal – €27,796,361.83
* Lowest Sales Country: Italy – €935,569.30
* Total Revenue: €113,361,738.71

### Data Insight

Sales are concentrated in a few countries. Portugal, France, and Sweden contribute a significant portion of total revenue, whereas countries like Italy and Spain generate comparatively lower sales.

### Business Recommendation

Maintain investment in high-performing countries while investigating opportunities to increase market penetration in low-performing regions.

### Supporting Visualization
Chart available in the Excel Dashboard.

## Question 2: What Product Categories Are Most Profitable?

### Finding

Clothing is the most profitable category, while Other is the least profitable.

### Key Metrics

* Total Categories: 10
* Highest Profit Category: Clothing – €2,941,938.09
* Second Highest: Games – €2,559,795.77
* Lowest Profit Category: Other – €549,033.71
* Total Profit: €18,992,427.72

### Data Insight

Profitability varies across categories. Clothing, Games, Appliances, and Electronics contribute the majority of profits.

### Business Recommendation

Increase focus on high-profit categories and evaluate the performance of low-profit categories to improve resource allocation.

### Supporting Visualization
Chart available in the Excel Dashboard.

## Question 4: What is the Relationship Between Device Type and Order Value?

### Finding

PC users have the highest average order value.

### Key Metrics by Device Type

| Device Type | Avg Order Value | Total Orders
| ----------- | --------------- | ------------
| Mobile      | €108,784.47     |     142
| PC          | €114,200.36     |     785
| Tablet      | €113,247.40     |     73

## Dataset Information

- Total Orders: 1000
- Countries: 15
- Product Categories: 10
- Sales Managers: 8
- Device Types: 3

### Data Insight

Customers using PCs spend slightly more per order than customers using Mobile devices. Tablet users have similar purchasing behavior to PC users.

### Business Recommendation

Continue optimizing the desktop purchasing experience while maintaining strong support for mobile and tablet users.

# Executive Summary

## Key Takeaways

1. Portugal is the highest-performing country in terms of revenue.
2. Clothing is the most profitable product category.
3. Celine Tumasian is the top-performing sales manager.
4. PC users have the highest average order value.

## Overall Business Performance

The business demonstrates strong sales and profitability with revenue concentrated among a few countries and managers. Product categories such as Clothing, Games, and Appliances contribute significantly to overall profitability. Device type has a modest impact on customer spending patterns.

## Top Priorities for Action

1. Expand business in underperforming countries.
2. Invest more heavily in high-profit product categories.
3. Replicate the strategies of top-performing sales managers.
4. Continue optimizing the desktop shopping experience.
